# VPMM_dataset
A multimodal dataset termed VPMM dataset is constructed for road pothole and manhole anomaly detection under complex lighting conditions. Based on the original RGB and infrared data, overexposed, low-light, and fused images with different proportions are generated to simulate degradation and hybrid perception scenarios in real-world road environments.

Dataset Description：
01 Visible Image：	The original RGB image captured under natural illumination, providing rich texture and appearance details.
02 Infrared Image：	The corresponding IR image (displayed in pseudo-color), reflecting thermal radiation differences.
03 High exposure：	The high-exposure enhanced image, used to simulate overexposure conditions or to emphasize bright regions.
04 Low brightness：	The low-brightness image, used to simulate low-light and shadowed environments.
05 Fusion Image： (25%IR + 75%RGB)	The fused image is generated by integrating 25% infrared information with 75% RGB natural-light information
06 Fusion Image： (50%IR + 50%RGB)	The fused image is generated by integrating 50% infrared information with 50% RGB natural-light information
07 Fusion Image： (75%IR + 25%RGB)	The fused image is generated by integrating 75% infrared information with 25% RGB natural-light information
08 labels ：labels of the dataset

All files of the VPMM_dataset will be made public upon acceptance of the paper.
